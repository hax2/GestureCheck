Human Gesture Rating

Rate short gesture videos for vocabulary learning

You will watch short videos and rate each gesture on three 1-5 scales. Use the target word shown with each video as the intended meaning.

Iconicity

How much the gesture visually resembles the meaning of the target word.

Sensorimotor Imagery

How strongly the gesture evokes bodily action, physical interaction, or perceptual experience.

Cultural Familiarity

How recognizable the gesture is in Western cultural contexts.
